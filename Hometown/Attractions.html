Attractions

People come from all over the world to visit this beautiful city not just for shopping or to go on walks. There are so many outdoor activities on Vancouver Island the residents are known to be some of the most active people in Canada. There’s ziplining between trees on the side of a mountain. Mini golfing in dense forests. Intense hikes that pay off with some of the most stunning views.

Victoria being an island in the Pacific Ocean, whale watching has also become quite popular among tourists. The Inner harbor is always filled with popup shops selling hand made goods you can’t get anywhere else. During the warmer seasons you might find very talented buskers performing stunt shows or dressed as Darth Vader playing the violin.
