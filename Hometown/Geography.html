Geography

Victoria is known for its scenery, with over 250 parks making up around 11% of the island. If parks aren’t your thing Vancouver Island also has a plethora of hiking trails. These trails vary in terrain types such as full-fledged mountain climbs, dense forest walks or coast side beach walks. Some of these trails include all three of these terrains for an all-in-one experience.

If you’re a fan of 17th century architecture look no further than the astonishing view of the city from Craigdarroch castle built in 1887. Take a stroll through the downtown area and find all kinds of hidden gems and while your down there take a walk-through Chinatown to absorb the culture and history.
